Catering On the go

We are a cafe that accept any kind of parties. We provide catering services on the go for breakfast, lunch and dinner. The cafe is located at Nayandanahalli, Bengaluru. We accept orders from any part of Banglore. The main goal of the cafe :

  • To provide delicious food for parties.
  • Providing food to IT companies over Bangalore.
  • Providing the food on time to the customers.
  • Providing real taste of ingredients without the use of chemicals.
